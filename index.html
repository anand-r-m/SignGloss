Why SignGloss?

Real-Time Translation

Signs are detected and translated in under 200ms using a sliding window of 64 frames. No lag, no waiting.

100% Client-Side

Everything runs in your browser — camera, AI model, and decoding. No data is ever sent to any server.

Indian Sign Language

Trained specifically for ISL with 600 gloss classes covering common signs, phrases, and everyday vocabulary.

GPU Accelerated

MediaPipe runs on your GPU for fast landmark extraction. ONNX Runtime uses optimized WASM for inference.
